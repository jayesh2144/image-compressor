Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a limit to the PDF size I can compress?

Our tool can handle PDFs up to 50MB in size. For best results with large PDFs, we recommend splitting them first.

Will my PDFs be stored on your servers?

No, all PDF processing happens in your browser. We don't store or upload your documents to any server.

Which compression level should I choose?

Medium compression is recommended for most documents. Use low compression for maximum quality, or high compression for smallest file size.
