How Our PDF Splitter Works

Our tool allows you to easily split PDF documents in several ways. Here's how to use it:

  1. Upload your PDF file by dragging and dropping or clicking the upload area
  2. Choose how you want to split your PDF (by pages, ranges, or custom selection)
  3. Click "Split PDF" to process your file
  4. Download the resulting PDF files individually or as a ZIP archive

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a limit to the PDF size I can split?

Our tool can handle PDFs up to 50MB in size. For best results with large PDFs, we recommend compressing them first.

Will my PDF be stored on your servers?

No, all PDF processing happens in your browser. We don't store or upload your documents to any server.

Can I split multiple PDFs at once?

Currently, our tool processes one PDF at a time for splitting. You can upload multiple files sequentially.

What's the difference between the split methods?

You can choose to split by: single pages (each page becomes a file), page ranges (group pages together), fixed intervals (every N pages), or custom page selection.
