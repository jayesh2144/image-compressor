Frequently Asked Questions

Will my PDF formatting be preserved?

Yes! Our converter maintains the original layout including fonts, images, and tables when possible.

Can I convert scanned PDFs?

Yes, use the OCR options to convert scanned PDFs to editable text. Accurate OCR works best but takes longer.

Is there a limit to the PDF size?

We recommend PDFs under 50MB for best performance. Very large files may take longer to process.
